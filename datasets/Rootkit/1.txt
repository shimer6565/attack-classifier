1. Kernel-Level Rootkit Detection
Alert Description: Discovery of unauthorized modifications to the system kernel, indicative of a kernel-level rootkit.
Triage Steps:
Immediately isolate affected systems to prevent further contamination.
Use specialized rootkit detection tools to confirm the presence and scope of the rootkit.
Reinstall the operating system from a known good source to remove the rootkit.
Update all security patches post-reinstallation and monitor for re-infection.
