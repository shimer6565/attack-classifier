4. Unusual Microphone Access
Alert Description: Unauthorized and unnoticed access to device microphones detected, likely for eavesdropping purposes.
Triage Steps:
Review which applications have microphone permissions and revoke unnecessary ones.
Scan the system for spyware and other malicious programs.
Update privacy settings on devices to limit app permissions.
Investigate the origin of the spyware and assess the scope of potential data breaches.
