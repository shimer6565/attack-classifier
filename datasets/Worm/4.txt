4. Unusual Account Lockouts
Alert Description: Multiple user accounts locked out due to failed login attempts, which may be due to a worm trying to spread via brute force attacks.
Triage Steps:
Review account lockout policies and adjust if necessary to mitigate disruptions.
Investigate the source of failed login attempts.
Reset passwords for affected accounts after ensuring systems are clean.
Implement account behavior monitoring to detect anomalies in the future.
